Lord you are my everything

Aniceto Nazareth

Lord, you are my everything,
Lord, you are my friend.
In this Bread You promise me:
Life that has no end.

Come, my strength, my Bread of life,
Hold me in Your sway.
Fill me with Your presence, Lord,
Guide me on my way.
